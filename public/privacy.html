Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-08

1. What we collect

We collect basic account information (email, display name) and content metadata you choose to manage in EasyPost. We may store generated previews and related analytics to operate the service.

2. How we use data

We use data to provide and improve EasyPost: authenticate users, store configurations, generate content, send Slack notifications, and analyze performance. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Storage & security

Data is stored in Supabase (Postgres + Storage). Access is limited by authentication and service credentials. While we implement safeguards, no method is 100% secure.

4. Third parties

We share data with service providers only as needed to operate EasyPost (e.g., Supabase, Slack). Their use is governed by their own policies.

5. Your choices

You may sign out, request deletion of content you control, or contact us to request account removal.

6. Data retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the service and meet legal obligations. Preview batches may expire automatically after a set period.

7. Children

EasyPost is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a minor provided personal data without consent, contact us to remove it.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available on this page.

9. Contact

Questions? Email support@easypost.fun
