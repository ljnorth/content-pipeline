How do I onboard an account?

Go to Accounts → fill the form → toggle Auto‑Gen to ON. You’ll get content daily at 5am ET in Slack.

How do I reroll images?

Open the Preview link in Slack. Select images → click “Replace Selected”.

How do I run ingestion now?

Use the “Run Ingestion (All)” button on Accounts, or per-account “Ingest”. Weekly ingestion runs automatically.
